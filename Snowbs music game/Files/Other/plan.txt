Text based game, no GUI
Account creation system 
play a random song from a list of 20
Tells you the list of 20
Popular songs that pople would know
You can select what group of songs you want, e.g. list of rock songs, pop and so on
3 points if right on first go
2 points if right on second 
1 point if right on third
Goes onto next song
if you dont get it right for four songs then you fail 
the score board is shown at end
written in C++
